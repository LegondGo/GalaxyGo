Announcement Center

Learn how to get your news and announcements featured on GalaxyGo

ÔøΩ How to Submit News for Publication

Want to share news, events, or announcements with the GalaxyGo community? Here's how to get your content featured in our News section:

Step 1: Prepare Your Content

  • Title: Create a clear, engaging headline
  • Description: Write a detailed explanation of your news/event
  • Links: Include any relevant websites, tournament pages, or resources
  • Images: Provide any relevant images or graphics (optional)

Step 2: Contact Our Moderators

Submit your content by contacting:

Primary Contact: Legond on online-go.com

Send a private message with your news submission

Step 3: Review Process

  • Our moderators will review your submission within 24-48 hours
  • Content must be Go-related and appropriate for the community
  • We may request minor edits or clarifications
  • Once approved, your news will appear in the News section

‚úÖ Content Guidelines

What We Accept:

  • Tournament announcements and results
  • Go-related events and workshops
  • Community achievements and milestones
  • Educational content and resources
  • Club and organization news

What We Don't Accept:

  • Commercial advertisements (unless community-relevant)
  • Off-topic content unrelated to Go
  • Inappropriate or offensive material
  • Spam or repetitive submissions

üí° Tips for Better Submissions

  • Be Specific: Include dates, times, and locations for events
  • Add Context: Explain why this news matters to the Go community
  • Proofread: Check for spelling and grammar before submitting
  • Include Sources: Link to official websites or announcements
  • Be Patient: Allow time for the review process
