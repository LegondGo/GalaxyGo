👨‍🏫 Looking to become a Tutor?

To apply as a tutor for the GalaxyGo community:

  1. Contact Legond on OGS
  2. Provide your Go background and teaching experience
  3. Demonstrate your commitment to helping others improve
  4. Complete a brief interview process

Tutor Benefits:

  • Help grow the GalaxyGo community
  • Share your passion for Go
  • Build lasting connections with students
  • Recognition within the community
